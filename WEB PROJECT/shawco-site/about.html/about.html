About SHAWCO

SHAWCO (Students' Health & Welfare Centres Organisation) is a student-run NGO at the University of Cape Town focused on improving quality of life in under-served communities through health and education initiatives.

Mission

To support community development by providing healthcare outreach and educational support programmes run by students and community members.
